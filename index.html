Xpense

Take control of your finances and achieve your financial goals with Xpense

Xpense is the ultimate personal finance management app. With a range of powerful features and a user-friendly interface, Xpense makes it effortless to manage your money, track your expenses, and gain valuable insights into your financial habits.

Key Features:

  • Track expenses, income, and inter-account transfers.
  • Manage multiple accounts (Debit, credit, wallet, cash, and investment).
  • Theming: Light and dark theme. Material You integrated (App's theme changes based on your phone's wallpaper).
  • Create and stay on top of your budgets.
  • Backup your data to Google Drive.
